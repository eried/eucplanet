EUC Planet HUD

A helmet display that mirrors your phone over WiFi.

Download HUD APK

Install

  1. Sideload hud-<version>.apk on the HUD device. Works on MotoEye E6, generic tablets, spare phones.
  2. Install phone-<version>-slim.apk on your phone, or get it from Google Play.

Pair

  1. Turn on the phone's hotspot. Connect the HUD to it.
  2. Launch the HUD app. It shows its IP on the splash.
  3. On the phone, open Settings → Integrations → MotoEye HUD, enter that IP, toggle the link on.

Telemetry starts within a second. Use matching versions on both sides.
