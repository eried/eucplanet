EUCPlanet

A no-nonsense, open-source app for electric unicycles.

Free, open source, no ads, no tracking, no upselling, no subscriptions, no telemetry phoned home, no nonsense.

Live telemetry, turn-by-turn navigation, a video recorder with on-screen telemetry overlay, a Wear OS dial, and a trip recorder. Modern, fast, all in one app.

A look inside

On the wrist

Built different

Turn-by-turn navigator

Multi-stop route builder with live guidance, off-route reroute, and a treasure-hunt mode for unmarked spots. Walk, bike, car, or straight-line. New Full Path mode lets you tap any point along the route to add a detour (sand, gravel, the wrong side of a one-way).

Overlay Studio

Record video from the phone camera with a tunable on-screen telemetry overlay: speed dial, battery, PWM, GPS map, custom layout. Export plain MP4 or transparent overlay for editing on top of footage from another camera.

Modern & fast

Pure black dark theme, low latency, built for glances while riding.

Private by default

No call-home, no analytics, no beacons. Rides stay on your device.

Flic 2 ready

Physical buttons for horn, lights, lock, voice. Eyes on the road.

Voice & alarms

Spoken reports and thresholds for speed, battery, PWM. In your language.

Trip recording & compare

GPS + telemetry exported as DarknessBot-compatible CSV. Overlay any past ride on the dashboard chart to compare speed, current, temperature side by side.

External GPS support

Pair a RaceBox or similar BLE GPS for centimetre-class speed and altitude without draining the phone radio.

Multi-wheel

V14 + P6 verified, V12 preliminary, with preview adapters for KingSong, Begode, Veteran, the InMotion V1 family, and Ninebot. Help us add yours. Already on the list but acting up? send a diagnostics recording.

Wear OS companion

Watch app for Galaxy Watch Ultra and other Wear OS 5+ watches. Phone holds the link, watch shows live speed, batteries, navigation cues, and quick controls (horn / light / voice).

Helmet HUD

Mirror the dashboard onto a MotoEye E6 or any Android-based HUD. Streams live telemetry from the phone over local WiFi. Sideload the small HUD APK on the visor display, point it at your phone, done.

Garmin Edge data field

Connect IQ data field for Edge bike computers. See EUC speed, battery and PWM on the bar-mounted Garmin while you ride. Bundled .iq supports Edge 530/540/830/840/1030/1040/1050 and more.

Varia rear-view radar

Pair a Garmin Varia RTL515 / RCT715 and get rear-vehicle warnings on the dashboard, the HUD, and the watch. Same sensor cyclists use, now wired into your EUC.
