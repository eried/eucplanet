EUC Planet

A no-nonsense, open-source Android app for electric unicycles.

Download APK View on GitHub

Why EUC Planet?

Built by a rider, for riders. No subscriptions, no accounts, and nothing phoning home behind your back — your rides aren't shipped off to some dashboard on the other side of the world. The app is fully open-source and driven by the community: features land because people asked for them and built them, not because a roadmap said so.

It starts with the InMotion V14, but the architecture is wheel-agnostic. Support for other unicycles is on the way.

Built different

Modern & fast

Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Material 3. Pure black dark theme. Designed for low latency and quick glances while riding.

Private by default

No call-home, no analytics pings, no hidden beacons. Your rides stay on your device — full stop.

Flic 2 ready

Map physical buttons to horn, lights, lock, voice reports, and more — hands stay on the bars.

Voice & alarms

Configurable spoken reports and threshold alarms for speed, battery, and PWM — in your language.

Trip recording

GPS + wheel telemetry exported as DarknessBot-compatible CSV. Own your data.

Wheel-agnostic

V14 first, but the BLE and UI layers are designed to grow. PRs welcome for other wheels.

Get involved

Found a bug? Want a feature? Ride a different wheel and want it supported? Open an issue or a pull request on GitHub. This is a community project — every contribution shapes where it goes next.
