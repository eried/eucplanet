EUC Planet

A no-nonsense, open-source app for electric unicycles.

Download APK Source code

Free, open source, no ads, no tracking, no upselling, no subscriptions, no telemetry phoned home, no nonsense. Live telemetry, turn-by-turn navigation, an on-screen telemetry overlay for video recording, a Wear OS dial, and a trip recorder, all in the same app. Tested on the InMotion V14 and P6, with preliminary V12 support and preview adapters for KingSong, Veteran, Begode (including high-voltage wheels), the InMotion V1 family, and Ninebot.

A look inside

On the wrist

Built different

Turn-by-turn navigator

Multi-stop route builder with live guidance, off-route reroute, treasure-hunt mode, and an external-routing-friendly OSRM endpoint. Walk, bike, car, or straight-line — pick a mode, drop your stops, ride.

Overlay Studio

Record video from the phone camera with a tunable on-screen telemetry overlay: speed dial, battery, PWM, GPS map, custom layout. Export plain MP4 or transparent overlay for editing on top of footage from another camera.

Modern & fast

Pure black dark theme, low latency, built for glances while riding.

Private by default

No call-home, no analytics, no beacons. Rides stay on your device.

Flic 2 ready

Physical buttons for horn, lights, lock, voice. Hands on the bars.

Voice & alarms

Spoken reports and thresholds for speed, battery, PWM. In your language.

Trip recording & compare

GPS + telemetry exported as DarknessBot-compatible CSV. Overlay any past ride on the dashboard chart to compare speed, current, temperature side by side.

External GPS support

Pair a RaceBox or similar BLE GPS for centimetre-class speed and altitude without draining the phone radio.

Multi-wheel

V14 + P6 verified, V12 preliminary, with preview adapters for KingSong (KS / S / F series), Begode (Master / MSX / RS / XWay / T-series), Veteran (Sherman / Patton / Lynx), InMotion V1 family, and Ninebot Z plus legacy. Help us add yours.

Wear OS companion

Watch app for Galaxy Watch Ultra and other Wear OS 5+ watches. Phone holds the link, watch shows live speed, batteries, navigation cues, and quick controls (horn / light / voice).
